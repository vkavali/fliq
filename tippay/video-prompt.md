# Video Generation Prompt for Fliq LinkedIn Launch

## Recommended Tools (ranked)

1. **Canva AI Video** (canva.com) — Best for LinkedIn. Templates, text animations, mockups. Free tier works.
2. **CapCut** — Great for short-form video with text overlays and transitions. Free.
3. **Synthesia** — AI avatar presenter if you want a talking-head style. Paid.
4. **Runway ML Gen-3** — Best pure AI video generation. Paid.
5. **Google Vids (Gemini)** — Works if you have Workspace. Slide-to-video.
6. **InVideo AI** — Type a script, get a video. invideo.io. Has free tier.

## Best for LinkedIn specifically: **Canva** or **InVideo AI**

## Prompt for AI Video Generators

Use this prompt in **InVideo AI**, **Canva Magic Studio**, or any text-to-video tool:

---

Create a 45-60 second professional product launch video for a fintech startup called "Fliq" — a UPI-native digital tipping platform for India.

**Visual style:** Clean, modern, minimal. Purple (#6C5CE7) as primary color, white backgrounds, subtle gradients. Think Stripe or Razorpay's visual style. Use smooth transitions and gentle zoom animations.

**Scene breakdown:**

Scene 1 (0-5s): Hook
- Text on screen: "Cash tips are disappearing."
- Background: Blurred image of a restaurant/cafe
- Quick fade to black

Scene 2 (5-12s): Problem
- Text: "India went digital. Tipping didn't."
- Show icons/visuals of UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Text: "Service workers are losing out."

Scene 3 (12-18s): Solution reveal
- Fliq logo animates in (purple rounded square with ₹ symbol)
- Text: "Introducing Fliq"
- Tagline appears: "Scan. Fliq. Done."

Scene 4 (18-35s): How it works (3 steps)
- Step 1: Show a QR code being scanned on a phone
  - Text: "Scan the QR code"
- Step 2: Show a tip amount selection screen (₹20, ₹50, ₹100)
  - Text: "Choose an amount"
- Step 3: Show UPI payment completion with a green checkmark
  - Text: "Pay via any UPI app"
- Each step transitions smoothly with a slide animation

Scene 5 (35-45s): Key features (quick cuts)
- "No app download needed" — icon of a phone with an X over app store
- "Zero commission under ₹100" — icon of 0% badge
- "Instant settlement" — icon of money reaching a bank
- "Free QR codes" — icon of QR code printing

Scene 6 (45-55s): Call to action
- Text: "Now in Beta"
- Green beta badge
- "Sign up at fliq.co.in"
- "DM us to join the beta program"
- Show the Fliq logo one more time

Scene 7 (55-60s): End card
- Fliq logo centered
- "Scan. Fliq. Done."
- Website: fliq.co.in
- Purple gradient background

**Music:** Upbeat, modern, corporate but not boring. Think tech startup energy.

**Text style:** Inter or similar clean sans-serif font. White text on colored backgrounds, dark text on white backgrounds.

**Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square) for LinkedIn feed, or 9:16 for LinkedIn Stories/Reels.
