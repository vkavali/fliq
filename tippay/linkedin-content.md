## About Section

Fliq is India's first UPI-native tipping platform. We make it effortless for customers to tip service providers — no app download, no registration. Just scan a QR code, pick an amount, and pay via any UPI app.

For providers — delivery drivers, waiters, salon stylists, hotel staff — Fliq means a free QR code, a real-time earnings dashboard, and instant payouts to their bank account.

Zero commission on tips up to Rs 100. Built on Razorpay. Currently in beta.

## First LinkedIn Post (Beta Launch Announcement)

**Post text:**

We just launched Fliq in beta.

Fliq is a UPI-native tipping platform built for India. Here's the problem we're solving:

Cash tips are disappearing. India moved to digital payments, but tipping didn't follow. Service workers — delivery drivers, waiters, barbers, hotel staff — are losing out.

Fliq fixes this in 3 steps:
1. Provider gets a free QR code
2. Customer scans it (no app needed)
3. Tips instantly via GPay, PhonePe, or any UPI app

What makes Fliq different:
- Zero friction for customers — no download, no signup
- Zero commission on tips up to Rs 100
- Instant settlement to provider's bank
- Earnings dashboard with ratings, streaks, and badges
- WhatsApp tip links for sharing

We're looking for beta testers — both providers and businesses.

If you run a restaurant, salon, hotel, or delivery service and want to try Fliq for your team, DM me or sign up at https://fliq.co.in

Built with NestJS, Flutter, Razorpay, and PostgreSQL.
Building in public. More updates coming.
