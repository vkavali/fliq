Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026

Fliq ("we", "us", "our") operates the Fliq mobile application and website at fliq.co.in (collectively, the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian laws.

Plain language summary: We collect only what we need (phone number, name, email) to let you send and receive digital tips. Razorpay handles all payment card/UPI data — we never see or store it. You can delete your account and data anytime.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Service delivery: Authenticating your identity via OTP, processing tip transactions, sending notifications
  • Communication: OTP codes via WhatsApp/SMS, tip received alerts, payout notifications
  • Improvement: Aggregated, anonymised analytics to improve the Platform (no individual profiling)
  • Legal compliance: Tax reporting, fraud prevention, responding to lawful requests from authorities

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage location: Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Railway / PostgreSQL) with encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Encryption: Sensitive fields (PAN, bank account numbers) are encrypted at the application level before storage
  • Access controls: Database access is restricted to authorised backend services only; no direct public access
  • Passwords: We use OTP-based authentication — no passwords are stored

4. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Your personal data may be stored on servers located outside India (currently in the United States). Under Section 16 of the DPDP Act, 2023, cross-border transfer of personal data is permitted to all countries except those specifically restricted by the Central Government. As of the effective date of this policy, no such restriction applies to the United States.

Payment data (card numbers, UPI details) is handled exclusively by Razorpay and stored in India in compliance with the RBI's data localisation directive (Circular DPSS.CO.OD No.2785/06.08.005/2017-2018).

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you and how it is processed
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal data through the app's profile settings or by contacting us
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time (this may affect your ability to use the Platform)
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File a complaint with our Grievance Officer or escalate to the Data Protection Board of India
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise your data rights in case of death or incapacity
