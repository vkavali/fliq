Delete Your Fliq Account

Your data, your choice.

We respect your right to erasure under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. You can request complete deletion of your Fliq account and all associated personal data.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (name, phone number, email, avatar, bio)
  • Your preferences (language, notification settings, WhatsApp opt-in)
  • Your FCM push notification token
  • Your QR codes and payment links (providers)
  • Your OTP history

What We Retain (Legal Requirement)

Transaction records (tip amounts, dates, provider/customer IDs) are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax and financial regulations. These records are anonymised — your name, phone, and email are removed.
This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, you cannot recover your profile, tip history, or provider earnings data. Any pending payouts will be processed before deletion.

How to Delete

Send an email from the phone number or email associated with your Fliq account:

Request Account Deletion

Or email delete@fliq.co.in with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The phone number or email on your Fliq account

Timeline

  • Acknowledgement: Within 48 hours
  • Pending payouts: Processed within 7 business days
  • Data deletion: Completed within 30 days of request
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email/SMS confirming deletion
